Quick Setup

Install Life Architect

Experience the full native performance. Install the app instantly without any app store hurdles.

1

Open Safari

Open Safari on your iOS device and head to life-architect.pages.dev. Note: Chrome on iOS does not support home screen apps.

Open App Link ↗
2

Tap the Share Button

Tap the Share icon at the bottom center of the Safari menubar.

Look for this Share icon
3

Select "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down the share menu list and tap Add to Home Screen, then tap Add in the top right corner.

The app will now behave perfectly natively from your home screen!
1

Open Chrome

Open Google Chrome and navigate to life-architect.pages.dev.

Open App Link ↗
2

Tap the Menu

Tap the 3-dot menu icon in the top right corner of the browser.

3

Install App

Select "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen" from the menu and confirm.

✨ The app is now installed and accessible from your app drawer!
1

Open App in Safari or Chrome

Go to life-architect.pages.dev in your preferred browser.

Open App Link ↗
2

If using Safari

Click File in the top menu bar, then click "Add to Dock".

3

If using Chrome

Look for the Install Icon (it looks like a screen with a down arrow) in the right side of the URL bar and click it.

Click the install icon in your address bar
1

Open Chrome or Edge

Navigate to life-architect.pages.dev.

Open App Link ↗
2

Click the Install Icon

Look at the far right of your address bar. You will see an App Available or Install icon.

Located directly inside your URL search bar
3

Pin to Taskbar

Once installed, right click the app icon in your taskbar and select "Pin to taskbar" for instant access.
